HEALTH & FITNESS

No matter what you do in life, your physical and mental fitness undoubtedly determines how good you can be. As individuals grow older, their number of responsibilities they have tend to grow with them. Whatever happens, don't neglect the most important part of your life.

FINANCE

Numbers make the world go round and everyone needs to have, at the very least, a basic understanding of Personal Finance. You don't need to know how to calculate the NPV and IRR of various investments and projects but you do need to understand what APR's are and how they can affect your retirement 10, 20, 30, or 40 years down the line.

RENEWABLE ENERGY

The world's water supply is vanishing at an unprecedented rate. Scientist aren't sure if we can reverse all the damage we've caused but we might as well try. Do me a favor and turn off the lights next time you leave a room.

REAL ESTATE

In terms of pure beauty, nothing comes close to the natural and historic wonders of the world. Next are the man-made wonders and that is the category I would lump architectural beauty into. There is nothing better than walking down the streets of Georgetown, DC, observing all of the pre-historic row homes and taking a stroll over the C&O canal. nature is the most beautiful thing of all but Architecture and Real Estate are a close second.
